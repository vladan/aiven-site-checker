chweb - Check websites state¶

CHWEB is a website checking tool.

ATM in its very early stages meant to demo aiven’s platform, using their kafka and postgresql services.

Features¶

  • Easy and intuitive YAML configuration.

  • Command line executables for the checker and the consumer/psql writer: chweb_collect and chweb_consume.

  • Environment configuration.
